## 🎬 Prompt Tạo Video

### Video Prompt Template (30-60 giây)

**Dành cho Runway ML / Pika Labs:**

```
Create a professional cooking video (45 seconds) showing Vietnamese [DISH_NAME]:

SCENE 1 (0-3 seconds):
- Wide shot of finished dish in bowl/plate
- Dish looks appealing with steam rising
- Recipe title appears

SCENE 2 (3-8 seconds): 
- Arrange all ingredients neatly on counter
- Show each ingredient close-up
- Professional kitchen lighting

SCENE 3 (8-25 seconds):
- Time-lapse of cooking process
- Show main preparation steps
- Hands adding ingredients
- Cooking/heating process
- Stirring and mixing

SCENE 4 (25-40 seconds):
- Final plating
- Adding garnish
- Arranging on serving dish
- Final touches

SCENE 5 (40-45 seconds):
- Beautiful close-up of finished dish
- Garnish details
- Professional food styling
- Call to action text appears

STYLE: Professional culinary video, warm colors, smooth transitions, 
food magazine quality, 4K resolution, appetizing food styling
```

## ⚡ Tips để Prompt Tốt Hơn

1. **Cụ thể hóa chi tiết:**
   - ✅ "bowl of pho with steam rising"
   - ❌ "pho"

2. **Thêm style descriptors:**
   - "professional food photography" 
   - "studio lighting"
   - "magazine quality"
   - "cinematic"

3. **Chất lượng cụ thể:**
   - Midjourney: `--quality 2` hoặc `--quality 1`
   - DALL-E: Chọn "Vivid" hoặc "Natural"
   - Stable Diffusion: Đặt seed để consistancy

4. **Tránh gì:**
   - ❌ Quá ngắn gọn
   - ❌ Mô tả không rõ ràng
   - ❌ Yêu cầu quá phức tạp
   - ❌ Spelling errors

5. **Tối ưu hóa:**
   - Bắt đầu đơn giản, sau đó refine
   - Tạo 3-4 variant, chọn cái tốt nhất
   - Sử dụng upscale features
   - Download chất lượng cao nhất
